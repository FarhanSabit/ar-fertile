Cheleted Zinc

Chelated zinc is a type of zinc supplement. It contains zinc that’s been attached to a chelating agent. Chelating agents are chemical compounds that bond with metal ions (such as zinc) to create a stable, water-soluble product that can be easily absorbed by the body.
