About Us

We feel privilege and pleasure to introduce AR FERTILIZER LTD, is a young and innovative ar fertilizer ltd company with a strong player in global feed additives business in Bangladesh market. We are manufactures of poultry and Fish feed, distributor importer and supplier of Feed additives,Ar fertilizer Ltd, Animal health care, poultry health care, poultry feed Supplement Company in Bangladesh. Our basic products are Coccidiostate, Choline, Choloride 60%, Fat emulsifier, Toxin Binder, non-antibiotic growth promoter, antioxidant, MCP, DCP, Phytase, Enzyme, Lysine, Methionine, vitamins premixes, organic trace minerals etc.

AR FERTILIZER LTD Started business in Bangladesh at the year 2010 and the CEO of The Company Mr. Atair Rahman has vast knowledge in poultry and Feed Sector in Bangladesh. His educational qualification is M.Sc (A.H) in poultry science. He started his first career in Biman poultry complex as a production officer at the year 1987 and left Biman poultry complex as a Farm Manager at the year 2005. He visited many countries in the world such as USA, Canada, England, Ireland, Holland, Belgium , Germany, Italy, Austria, Spain, Singapore , Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, India, Pakistan may times to attend the poultry fair/Seminar/Symposium. Now Mr. Atiar Rahman is the Chairman of Agrotech Feeds Ltd and Managing Director of A R Animal Health Ltd. The principal of AR FERTILIZER LTD is to supply quality poultry products & Feed additives such as DCP. MCP. Choline Chloride, Fat Emusifier, Coccidiostat, Feed Enzymes, Feed Phytase, Lysine DL-Methionie, Toxin Binder , Pellet Binder etc with minimum profit to boost up the poultry and feed industry in Bangladesh.
